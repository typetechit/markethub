Those requested in each personal data collection form and at least the following:

  • Contact data: e-mail.
  • Data on food intolerance and allergies, and disabilities.
  • Data related to the request or application provided by the user.
  • Flight information.

Respond to the request for registration in the event as well as all questions related to this and requests, queries or complaints through the sections and contact forms on the Platform or through other channels such as email for general information on the Platform

The processing of your personal data related to the management of the registration to the event is legitimate as it is necessary to comply with the contractual or pre-contractual obligations arising from the relationship between HOTELBEDS and you.

When complaint forms are used, it shall be based on the legal obligation in accordance with the regulations for the defense of consumers and users
